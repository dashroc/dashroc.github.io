An Extremely Honest “About Me” Section

I’m an experienced visual designer that's super interested in freelance and short-term contract work.

I’ve been labeled throughout my career as “easy to work with”, “a workhorse” and “fast”. All traits that I take pride in and fully expect myself to adhere to in any future endeavors. I’m quiet, but not shy. I may like to have a good think before I speak, but am perfectly capable of doing so. You can be sure that if I have a question, I’ll ask it and if I have something to state, I’ll say it.

I enjoy slipping my headphones on, putting my head down and hammering on high-fidelity mockups. I both prefer and excel at doing UI design, but I always approach it with a UX mindset.

I’ve held various titles that range from low to lofty and I can tell you that accolades are not why I do what I do. I’m interested in design work, plain and simple. I love solving visual design problems and I like getting shit done.

I can take direction as easily as doling out my opinion and I'm happy to do whichever is required of me. I’m also perfectly capable of working as part of a team or running solo.

Thank you in advance for your interest and taking the time to check me out!

Cordially,
