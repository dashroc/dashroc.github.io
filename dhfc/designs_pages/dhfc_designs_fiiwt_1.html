False Idols I Worship Thee - Issue I

Zine in which I list off my role models, heros, idols, crushes, etc. If you unfold the zine, the back features a list of songs to go with each image on each page. Listed in this Issue: Andy Warhol (), Henri Cartier-Bresson (), Evelyn Waugh (), Jean-Luc Godard (), Oscar Wilde (), Peter Sellers (). Materials/Tools: Paper, Label Maker, Typewriter, Photoshop, Inkjet Printer.
